CLAHub

CLAHub provides a low-friction way to have a Contributor License Agreement for your open source project that’s hosted on GitHub.

Contributors digitally sign your CLA by signing in with GitHub.

Then, it automatically marks up your pull requests based on whether the contributors have all signed your CLA.

OpenROV

OpenROV is a open-source underwater robot. But it’s so much more. It’s also a community of people who are working together to create more accessible, affordable, and awesome tools for underwater exploration.

The backbone of the project is the global community of DIY ocean explorers who are working, tinkering and improving the OpenROV design.
