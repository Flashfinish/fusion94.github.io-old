Wireless Local Number Portability (WLNP)

Dec 13th, 2003 Comments

Thank You Federal Communications Commission. Thanks to the Wireless Local Number Portability Mandate I’ve dropped SprintPCS and have moved over to Cingular. My number remains the same and was able to get a neat new phone as part of the switchover. I picked up a Sony Ericsson T616. So far it’s pretty sweet. The only major drawback I’ve seen so far is that entering contacts via the phone interface sucks. I’m going to have either buy a new Powerbook or buy a bluetooth card for my existing ti-book.
