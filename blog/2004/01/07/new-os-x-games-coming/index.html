New OS X Games Coming

Jan 7th, 2004 | Comments

It’s always good news when OS X gets a couple of popular PC Titles ported to it. Yesterday Aspyr announced that they’ll be publishing Call of Duty and Black Hawk Down. Looks like the release dates are in April/May Time frame.

Also to note is that Splinter Cell is currently in Alpha and hopefully will be released later this month.
