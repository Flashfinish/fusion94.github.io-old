“Mulligan’s for Mutts” Golf Tournament

Sep 16th, 2006

Thursday I had the opportunity to play in the “Mulligan’s for Mutts” golf tournament. We (the format was a four-man scramble) played over at StoneTree Golf Club over in Marin county and ended up in 7th place with a score of 70 (-2).

StoneTree is a difficult course and we were happy with our end result. Even better was the fact that approximately $15,000 was raised for the Second Chance Rescue Organization. If you are feeling charitable and have a few extra $$ head over there and make a contribution. They accept checks, cash and PayPal.
